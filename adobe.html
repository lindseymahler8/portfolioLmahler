Adobe Projects

The Adobe Creative Cloud is an important tool for all designers to learn and master. Throughout my years at James Madison University I have created and tested designs using the Creative Cloud. On this page, you can browse some of the projects I have created within and outside of the scope of my undergraduate degree. The applications I used included Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, and XD.

Illustrator

Bunkmates Logo

This logo was created in my SMAD 317: User Interaction Design class at James Madison University. For this project, we were to create a logo for a new app with a design problem. the design problem I chose was "How might we improve the experience of college students trying to find roommates or companions". This logo was the face of my user research project; which can be found in the XD section.

Dipped in the Burg Logo

This logo was created for my small business; Dipped in Burg. As Co-Owner of the business, we create chocolate customizable boxes for birthday's, holiday's, and events.

Finding Calm Focusing Logo

This logo was created in my SMAD 201: Foundations of Visual Communication Design class. We were to create a logo for a new business aimed at helping an issue with the COVID-19 pandemic. The logo I designed was for a virtual therapy company aimed at helping those who are at risk or too fearful to leave their homes. Part two of this project can be found in the InDesign section.

Pocket Librarian Logo

This logo was created in my SMAD 203: Foundations of User Experience Design class. We were to create a logo for our new app that fixes a design problem. The design problem I chose was "How might we improve the experience of JMU students trying to find affordable books". The user research to this project can be found in the XD section.

Photoshop

Soul Photoshop Project

This Photoshopped image was designed outside the scope of my studies at JMU but it was inspired by works I have learned. I designed the image with the question in mind: When you see a person, what best represents their soul? The photo is a picture of my mom, Beth. When I see my mother, I see calm, caring, and a shoulder to cry on. The best way I could represent these traits was by capturing an image of a beach with birds flying above. Using my knowledge of Photoshop, I created the image above called Soul by combining photos I took of my mom and the beech.

Time Photoshop Project

This Photoshopped image was created within my SMAD 201: Foundations of Visual Communication Design class. The goal of this project was to demonstrate the meaning of time within an image. What I did was set up my tripod in the same location and took pictures of myself in different positions. Once all the photos were taken, I was able to layer them and create one compacted image demonstrating the passing of time.

XD Design

Bunkmates User Research

This project was created in my SMAD 317: Interactive Design class. User Research allows for the designer to use suggestions from users instead of waiting for feedback after production. For my user research project, my group focused on the design problem; "College students need to find a better way to connect with other students when looking for roommates or friends with similar qualities as theirs". The button above is a link to our high-fidelity prototype we created using XD.
