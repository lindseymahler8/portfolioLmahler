Designed Websites

This page consists of Websites I have designed within the scope of my studies at James Madison University. Using HTML and CSS, I was able to create a business site, informational site, retail site, and research site. Each site design has allowed me to experience coding and better understand it for future career endeavors.

Business Website

Dipped in Burg Website

This project was created in my SMAD 307: Interactive Design for Web class. We were tasked with creating a site for a business of our choosing. Within the scope of the project, designers must have multiple pages and involve simple design elements. I chose to design a website for my small business, Dipped in the Burg. Our small business creates customizable chocolate heart boxes for birthdays, holidays, and special occasions.


Displayed are images of the home screen of my website. The home page includes elements like navigation, links to other pages, and a footer. It also hosts pictures of products and links to place future orders.

Research Website

User Research website

This project was created in my SMAD 307: Interactive Design for Web class. We were tasked with creating designing a website that followed our user research on an app of our choosing. Within the scope of the project, designers must have a one-page design and involve simple design elements. I chose to research the app, OurHouse. OurHouse is an app popularly used app among college sororities and fraternities to keep members updated on information, show proof of participation, and manage points throughout the semester. For my research, I surveyed 48 sorority and fraternity members through a google doc survey. This survey had questions to determine the main issues they experience while using the app and what can be changed to improve their experience. The major concern dealt with the check-in feature within the app.


Displayed are images of the home screen of my website. The home page includes elements like navigation and a full outline of my research.
