Get to know Lindsey Mahler

I was born and raised in Wayne, NJ with my mom Beth, my dad Mike, and two brothers: Josh and Reilly. Throughout my lifetime I have always been driven by my creativity and humor. Humor is a tool not everyone can possess, but if you do, it can be a great asset. For me, humor has been the perfect asset; allowing me to make meaningful relationships, gain confidence, and be the light in someone’s gloomy day. Not only has humor helped me in those ways, but it has also helped my creativity to blossom. Whether it was making up a creative visual art with Photography or a logo for the next upcoming app; I have always found ways to incorporate design into my life. When deciding on my major at James Madison, it only seemed fitting to choose Media Arts and Design. With more learning and experience to come, James Madison is only the beginning. I cannot wait to see what my future holds!
